PANCAKES RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • Plain / all purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Milk,full fat
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

STEPS FOR THE RECIPE

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the milk, oil and eg
  2. Combine the dry ingredients and add to the egg mixture. Whisk
  3. Grease a skillet or griddle
  4. Scoop the batter onto the skillet
  5. Heat until pancake bubbles and flip, until lightly golden brown
