SPONGE CAKE RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • Plain / all purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Milk,full fat
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract
  1. Mix the dry ingredients first. These are flour ,sugar, baking powder, salt .
  2. Mix the liquid ingredients nextthat include the eggs , milk , oil , vanilla essence
  3. Thoroughly mix all the ingredients until the mixture is smooth.Lastly add to the greased pan and shove into the preheated oven
